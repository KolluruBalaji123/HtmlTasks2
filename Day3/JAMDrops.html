Jam Drops

JamDrops ingredients:

  1. 1/2 cup butter, softened
  2. 1cup sugar
  3. 2 eggs
  4. 1/4teaspoon vanilla
  5. 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  6. 1 teaspoon baking soda
  7. 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  8. 1/4teaspoon salt
  9. 1 1/2 cups shredded peeled apples (about 2 medium apples)
  10. 1/2 cup chopped walnuts
  11. 1quart vanilla or cinnamon ice cream

How to prepare the JamDrops Shown in the bwlow:


  • Preheat oven to 180Â°C. Line 2 baking trays with non-stick baking paper. Use an electric beater to beat the butter , sugar and vanilla essence in a medium bowl until pale and creamy. Add the egg and beat until combined
  • Use lightly floured hands to roll teaspoonfuls of mixture into balls. Place on the prepared trays, about 5cm apart. Use a lightly floured finger to make an indentation in the centre of each ball.
  • Bake in preheated oven for 15 minutes, swapping trays halfway through cooking, or until the biscuits are cooked through and light golden. Remove biscuits from the oven
